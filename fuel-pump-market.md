<p><strong>The Fuel Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Common Fuel Pump</li><li>GDI Fuel Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel pump market is primarily divided into two types: common fuel pumps and Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI) fuel pumps. Common fuel pumps, typically found in traditional internal combustion engines, provide fuel at a steady pressure and are widely used in various vehicles. In contrast, GDI fuel pumps are designed for modern engines that inject fuel directly into the combustion chamber, enhancing efficiency and performance. This differentiation highlights the evolving technology in the automotive industry, catering to diverse engine requirements.</p></p>
