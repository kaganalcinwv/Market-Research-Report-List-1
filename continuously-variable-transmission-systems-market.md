<p><strong>The Continuously Variable Transmission Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Belt-Chain Drive CVT</li><li>Planetary CVT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The continuously variable transmission (CVT) systems market features two primary types: belt-chain drive CVT and planetary CVT. Belt-chain drive CVT utilizes a system of belts and pulleys to provide an infinite range of gear ratios, enhancing efficiency and smooth acceleration. In contrast, planetary CVT employs a gear arrangement consisting of sun, planet, and ring gears to achieve variable gear ratios with compact design and high torque capabilities. Both types are pivotal in improving vehicle performance and fuel efficiency across various applications.</p></p>
