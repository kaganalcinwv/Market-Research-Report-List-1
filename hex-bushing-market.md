<p><p>The Hex Bushing Market is categorized into two primary types: Fully Coupled and Screw Thread. Fully Coupled hex bushings feature a design that allows for direct, secure connection between two pipes, ensuring minimal leakage and maintaining pressure integrity. In contrast, Screw Thread hex bushings employ threaded connections, facilitating easy installation and disassembly. Both types serve critical roles in plumbing and industrial applications, providing versatile solutions for adapting pipe sizes while supporting efficient fluid flow and system reliability.</p></p>
