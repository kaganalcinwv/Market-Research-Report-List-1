<p><p>Meat forks are categorized into two primary market types: curved and straight. Curved meat forks feature a slightly arched design, which helps in lifting and serving meats while minimizing tearing. They are ideal for handling larger cuts and roasts. In contrast, straight meat forks have a long, straight prong design, offering precision and ease in piercing meat for carving or serving. Both types cater to different culinary needs, enhancing efficiency in cooking and serving meat dishes.</p></p>
