<p><p>The blank mask market encompasses various types used in photolithography for semiconductor manufacturing. Low Reflectance Chrome-film Blank Masks feature a chrome layer with minimal reflectance, enhancing imaging accuracy by reducing unwanted reflections that can distort features. On the other hand, Attenuated Phase Shift Blank Masks utilize phase-shifting techniques to improve resolution and contrast in patterning by manipulating the phase of light, allowing for finer details in chip design. Together, these masks play crucial roles in advanced semiconductor fabrication processes.</p></p>
