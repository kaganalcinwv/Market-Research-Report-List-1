<p><strong>The VOC Sensor Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC sensor device market encompasses various technologies, notably Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductors (MOS). PID sensors utilize ultraviolet light to ionize volatile organic compounds, providing highly sensitive real-time measurements suitable for industrial applications. In contrast, MOS sensors rely on changes in conductivity in metal-oxide films, offering cost-effective solutions for detecting common VOCs. Both types are crucial for environmental monitoring, ensuring indoor air quality, and supporting regulatory compliance across various sectors, including healthcare and manufacturing.</p></p>
