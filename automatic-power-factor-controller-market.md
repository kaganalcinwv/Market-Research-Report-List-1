<p><p>The Automatic Power Factor Controller (APFC) market consists of Active Power Factor Controllers and Passive Power Factor Controllers. Active Power Factor Controllers utilize advanced electronic components to dynamically adjust and improve power factor in real time, enhancing energy efficiency and reducing losses. In contrast, Passive Power Factor Controllers rely on fixed capacitors and inductors to manage reactive power, offering a more straightforward, cost-effective solution. Both types play crucial roles in industrial and commercial settings for optimizing electrical systems and reducing energy costs.</p></p>
