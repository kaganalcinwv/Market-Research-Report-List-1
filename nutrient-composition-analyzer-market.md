<p><p>The Nutrient Composition Analyzer market is primarily categorized into two types: DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) technology and NIR (Near-Infrared) technology. DEXA technology offers precise measurements of body composition, including fat and lean mass, making it essential in clinical research and healthcare. In contrast, NIR technology uses light absorption to analyze the nutrient content in food and feed products quickly and non-destructively, enabling efficient quality control in food industries. Both technologies play crucial roles in nutritional assessment and product development.</p></p>
