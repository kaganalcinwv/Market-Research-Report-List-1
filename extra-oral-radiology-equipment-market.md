<p><p>The Extra Oral Radiology Equipment market features two primary types: Panoramic X-ray Units and Cephalometric X-ray Units. Panoramic X-ray Units capture a broad view of the teeth, jaws, and surrounding structures in a single image, making them essential for diagnosing dental issues and planning treatments. Cephalometric X-ray Units focus on the side view of the head, providing crucial insights into jaw relationships and facial structures, commonly used in orthodontics and maxillofacial assessments. Both are vital for comprehensive dental imaging.</p></p>
