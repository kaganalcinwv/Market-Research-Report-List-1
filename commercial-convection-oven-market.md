<p><p>The commercial convection oven market includes two primary types: countertop convection ovens and floor model convection ovens. Countertop convection ovens are compact, portable units ideal for smaller kitchens or establishments with limited space, offering flexibility without sacrificing performance. In contrast, floor model convection ovens are larger, stationary units designed for high-capacity operations, suitable for restaurants and catering facilities. Both types utilize circulating hot air for even cooking, enhancing efficiency and food quality in commercial food preparation.</p></p>
