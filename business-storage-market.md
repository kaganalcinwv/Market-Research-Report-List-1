<p><p>The business storage market primarily consists of two types: non-climate controlled storage and climate-controlled storage. Non-climate controlled storage offers basic space for items that are not sensitive to temperature or humidity, making it a cost-effective option for businesses with general storage needs. In contrast, climate-controlled storage maintains specific temperature and humidity levels, safeguarding sensitive items such as electronics, artwork, or perishable goods. Both options cater to varying business requirements, ensuring flexibility in storage solutions.</p></p>
