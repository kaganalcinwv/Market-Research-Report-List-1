<p><strong>The Wireless Charging Integrated Circuits ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmitter IC</li><li>Receiver IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless charging integrated circuits (ICs) market is primarily divided into transmitter ICs and receiver ICs. Transmitter ICs are responsible for generating the electromagnetic field to initiate and control the charging process. They manage power delivery and communication with the receiver. Receiver ICs are designed to capture the electromagnetic energy and convert it into direct current to charge the device's battery. Both types are crucial for the efficiency, safety, and compatibility of wireless charging systems across various applications.</p></p>
