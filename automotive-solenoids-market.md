<p><p>The automotive solenoids market consists of various valve types that control fluid flow within vehicles. A 4-way valve enables fluid to flow in multiple directions, commonly used in systems like pneumatic controls. The 5-way valve offers additional flexibility, managing multiple flow paths, enhancing control in advanced automotive systems.</p></p>
