<p><p>The medical heat shrinkable tube market is primarily categorized into two types: fluoropolymer and polyolefin tubes. Fluoropolymer medical heat shrinkable tubes are known for their superior chemical resistance, temperature stability, and low friction properties, making them suitable for critical applications in healthcare. In contrast, polyolefin medical heat shrinkable tubes are more cost-effective, offering flexibility and good thermal properties, commonly used for insulation and protection in medical devices. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing the performance of medical equipment.</p></p>
