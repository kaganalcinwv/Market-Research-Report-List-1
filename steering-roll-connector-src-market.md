<p><p>The Steering Roll Connector (SRC) market encompasses three primary types: Spiral Wound Type, U-Turn Roller Type, and U-Turn Dummy Type. The Spiral Wound Type features a coiled design for enhanced flexibility and efficiency in steering operations. The U-Turn Roller Type utilizes rollers to facilitate smooth directional changes, improving maneuverability. The U-Turn Dummy Type, while lacking moving parts, serves as a placeholder or guide in steering systems. Each type caters to specific applications, influencing performance and operational dynamics in various vehicles.</p></p>
