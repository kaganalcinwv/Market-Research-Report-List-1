<p><p>Construction drawing management tools can be categorized into three market types: Installed-PC, Installed-Mobile, and Cloud-Based. Installed-PC tools require software installation on individual computers, allowing for robust offline functionality but limiting accessibility. Installed-Mobile solutions are designed for mobile devices, facilitating on-site access and collaboration, yet they may lack comprehensive features compared to PC versions. Cloud-Based tools offer real-time collaboration and accessibility from any internet-enabled device, enhancing team communication and efficiency while reducing IT maintenance burdens associated with installed solutions.</p></p>
