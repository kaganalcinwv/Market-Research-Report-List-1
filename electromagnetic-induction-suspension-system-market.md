<p><p>The Electromagnetic Induction Suspension System market is primarily divided into two types: active and passive electromagnetic suspension systems. Active electromagnetic suspension systems use sensors and control algorithms to adjust damping and stiffness in real-time, enhancing ride comfort and vehicle handling. In contrast, passive electromagnetic suspension systems rely on fixed magnetic fields and mechanical components to provide support and stability, offering a more cost-effective solution with simpler design but less adaptability to varying road conditions. Both systems aim to improve vehicle performance and passenger experience.</p></p>
