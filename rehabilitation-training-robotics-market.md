<p><p>The Rehabilitation Training Robotics market encompasses three main types: lower extremity, upper extremity, and exoskeleton systems. Lower extremity robotics focuses on enhancing mobility and strength in patients with leg and ankle impairments, often aiding in gait training. Upper extremity systems target rehabilitation of the arms and hands, assisting in restoring motor function and coordination. Exoskeletons provide external support for the entire body, enabling individuals with severe mobility limitations to regain independence and participate in rehabilitation exercises effectively.</p></p>
