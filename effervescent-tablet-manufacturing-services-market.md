<p><p>Effervescent tablet manufacturing services encompass different production methods tailored to optimize stability and release characteristics. Wet granulation involves combining ingredients with a liquid binder to form granules, enhancing tablet integrity and dissolution. Dry granulation compresses powder directly into granules without moisture, ideal for heat-sensitive materials. Direct compression utilizes pre-formed granules, allowing for efficient production with fewer processing steps. Each method serves unique formulation needs, influencing the tablet's performance, disintegration, and overall consumer experience in various applications.</p></p>
