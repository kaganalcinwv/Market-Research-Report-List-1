<p><p>The Acute Surgical Mechanical Circulatory Support System market comprises various devices designed to support or replace heart function in critically ill patients. Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO) devices provide life-sustaining support by oxygenating blood outside the body, crucial during severe respiratory or cardiac failure. Extracorporeal Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs) aid in pumping blood for patients with weakened hearts. Other devices in this market may include temporary balloon pumps and intra-aortic balloon pumps, utilized in specific acute cardiac conditions and surgical settings.</p></p>
