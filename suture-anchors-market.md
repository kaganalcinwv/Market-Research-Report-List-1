<p><p>The suture anchors market is primarily categorized into two types: UHMWPE (Ultra-High Molecular Weight Polyethylene) and titanium. UHMWPE anchors are favored for their lightweight, biocompatibility, and resistance to wear, making them ideal for soft tissue repairs. In contrast, titanium anchors offer superior strength, durability, and imaging visibility, often preferred in orthopedic applications. Both materials cater to distinct surgical needs, contributing to advancements in minimally invasive techniques and improving patient outcomes in tissue fixation procedures.</p></p>
