<p><p>The automotive exhaust components market encompasses several key types vital for vehicle performance and emissions control. Manifold mufflers reduce noise and facilitate exhaust flow. Clamps secure pipes and components, ensuring a leak-proof system. Stack pipes are used in heavy-duty applications for efficient exhaust venting. Manifold pipes connect the exhaust manifold to the rest of the system, directing gases away from the engine. Connectors join various exhaust parts, ensuring seamless integration and proper exhaust management, enhancing overall vehicle efficiency.</p></p>
