<p><strong>The Tuna Canning Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Canning Machine</li><li>Protective Gas Canning Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tuna canning machine market primarily consists of two types: vacuum canning machines and protective gas canning machines. Vacuum canning machines remove air from the can before sealing, which helps in preserving the tuna's freshness and extending shelf life. In contrast, protective gas canning machines use inert gases, such as nitrogen, to displace oxygen inside the can, preventing oxidation and maintaining quality. Both methods aim to enhance the preservation of tuna while ensuring safety and longevity in storage.</p></p>
