<p><p>Fiber Optic Plates (FOP) serve distinct purposes in optical applications, categorized mainly into straight and tapered types. Straight FOPs offer consistent light transmission, ideal for applications requiring uniformity, such as in imaging systems. Tapered FOPs, on the other hand, gradually change in cross-section, enabling efficient light coupling and focusing, making them suitable for high-resolution imaging and endoscopic applications. Both types leverage fiber optic technology to enhance image clarity and transmission efficiency across various fields, including medical and industrial uses.</p></p>
