<p><p>The industrial water treatment chemicals market comprises various types designed to enhance water quality and system efficiency. Antifoams reduce foam formation, while oxygen scavengers eliminate dissolved oxygen to prevent corrosion. Corrosion inhibitors protect metal surfaces, and biocides and disinfectants eliminate harmful microorganisms. Oxidants facilitate the removal of contaminants, and pH conditioners adjust acidity levels for optimal chemical reactions. Sludge conditioners enhance sludge handling, while scale inhibitors prevent mineral buildup in equipment, ensuring operational efficiency and longevity.</p></p>
