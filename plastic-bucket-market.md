<p><p>The plastic bucket market primarily features two types: High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) and Polypropylene (PP). HDPE buckets are known for their strength, resistance to impact, and UV stability, making them ideal for outdoor use and heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, PP buckets are more flexible and resistant to chemical corrosion, often used in food storage and industrial settings. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs, from household cleaning to industrial storage, driving demand across various sectors.</p></p>
